IEEE LaunchPad Kerala

One of the most prestigious events of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates. Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to propel advancement and innovation within Kerala's employment landscape by facilitating the seamless exchange of ideas and expertise.

Complete Event Roadmap

1) Online Registration: Candidates start by registering on the Launchpad Kerala website. Upon successful registration, they secure their place in the event.

2) Confirmation: Within 48 hours of registration, candidates will receive a confirmation email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team. This email will contain information about the next steps, which will also be available on the updates page of the website.

3) Join the Community: Candidates are encouraged to join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server, where they can participate in the Launchpad Challenges. Details for joining can be found on the provided GitHub link.

4) Technical Challenge: Once on the Mulearn Discord Server, candidates will find various levels and tasks under the IEEE Launchpad category in the #ieee-launchpad-announcement channel. The challenge is divided into three levels:
Level 1: Foundational Skills and Introduction
Level 2: Specialized Learning Paths and Challenges
Level 3:Industry-curated Challenges and Hackathons
Complete and submit their tasks on time to earn Karma points. Daily office hours for support will be held from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm in the #ieee-launchpad-officehours channel. Progress can be tracked using the digital identity in the #ieee-launchpad-leaderboard.

5) Online Aptitude Test: Candidates will then take an online assessment to showcase their competencies to potential employers.

6) Await the Finalized Results: Results will be announced after a thorough evaluation of candidates' performance in both the Technical Challenge and the online test. This evaluation will open up exciting opportunities based on their achievements.

7) Final Interview:The final stage is an interview, providing candidates with the chance to showcase their skills and make a lasting impression on recruiters. This step is crucial for securing their desired job.

For more details, connect with us in the Mulearn Discord channel.
